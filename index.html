About Hepatitis B

Hepatitis B is a liver-damaging virus. Infants are at unique risk because their immune systems are still developing.

The "Silent" Infection:
If an infant is infected at birth, 90 out of 100 will develop a lifelong (chronic) infection. Most will show no symptoms for decades until liver damage occurs.

The vaccine is over 90% effective at preventing this lifelong condition if started at birth.
